about

Hello, I'm Jia Xu, currently a MSCS student at Northeastern University Seattle Campus.

I used to be a Registered Structural Engineer in China for about 5 years. During my first career, I delivered more than 35 projects, including Hangars, Cargo Warehouses, Airship hangars, Office Buildings, Hotels, etc.. I was also the structural design leader of 2 Projects, including an extra-extra-large Hangar with a span of more than 120 meters and an area of more than 10,000 square meters.

I enjoy coding and the challenge of learning something new everyday. Switching my career to Computer Science gives me a change to explore a brand new field and technologies, and also work in an industry which I have been passionate about for a long time.
